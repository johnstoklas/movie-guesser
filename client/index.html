Instructions

Welcome to Movie Guesser. You must place 10 movies in the correct order, by release year, on your timeline.

If two movies have been released in the same year, either side will be accepted as a correct answer.

If you know the title of the movie, you can obtain steal tokens which can be used against your oppenent. If you believe they placed a movie in the wrong position, you can attempt to steal the movie from them by placing your steal token on their timeline.
